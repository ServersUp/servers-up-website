Game Server Status Alerts in Discord Without Spam

ServersUp team · July 19, 2026

Discord communities mute game-server status bots when alerts feel like noise. Useful alerts fire only when a realm flips UP or DOWN, for the servers you chose. That is the gap ServersUp is built to fill.

Why do game status bots get muted?

Guild and raid Discords care when a WoW or FFXIV realm comes back. People still refresh official status pages, or they add a bot that pings too often. When every rumor or every realm becomes a notification, the channel gets muted. The status feed stops being useful.

Official pages stay the source of record (for example Blizzard realm status). The problem is not finding status once. It is getting a clean signal in Discord without babysitting the page.

What does “at your convenience and configuration” mean?

ServersUp exists so communities get UP/DOWN when it matters, on their terms. You pick and choose which games and realms to follow. Alerts go where you configure them. You are not subscribed to every realm, every poll, or someone else’s idea of what is important.

Check the live server status board anytime without Discord. When you want channel alerts, install the bot and wire only what your group needs.

Tired of refreshing status pages? ServersUp can post when a realm you watch goes up or down. Set it up in about a minute.

FAQ

Do I have to watch every realm?

No. Choose the games and realms your community cares about. Skip the rest so Discord stays quiet until something you configured actually changes.

Do I need Discord just to check server status?

No. The live status board shows WoW and FFXIV realms without installing anything. Discord is optional when you want channel alerts.

Which games does ServersUp support today?

World of Warcraft and Final Fantasy XIV, across the regions listed on the live board and in the install guide.
